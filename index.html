About

I am currently an LLM Researcher at Techtouch. I received my Ph.D. in Informatics in March 2025 from Yokohama National University, where I was advised by Prof. Tatsunori Mori. My research centers on natural language processing — specifically information extraction, prompting, and language models.

I constructed the Japanese Information Extraction Mutual Reinforcement Effect Mix Dataset (covering sentence classification, text classification, sentiment classification, sentiment part-of-speech classification, relation extraction, and event extraction), and fine-tuned a number of Japanese pre-trained and large language models. You can find our papers, code, models, and datasets on Hugging Face and GitHub.
